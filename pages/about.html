I am a student at DeVry University working toward an Associates of Science in Information Technology and Networking. With a GPA of 3.7 I am looking forward to graduating in August of 2023.

I am also participating in the Cybersecurity Workforce Certification Training program, a certification based national training program for transitioning military, first responders, and other adult trainees. The CWCT prepares students to sit for CompTIA A+, Linux+, Security+, Red Hat Certified System Administrator, Cisco CyberOps Associate, and EC-Council Certified Ethical Hacker and is supported by a National Centers of Academic Excellence in Cybersecurity Grant, housed at the National Security Agency.
